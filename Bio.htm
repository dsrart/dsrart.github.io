Bio


Emerging visual artist. Born in Soviet Russia. Immigrated to the US in 2002. Based in Miami, FL.

After experimenting with photography on and off for a decade, last year I made an active switch to sculpture, installation, mixed media, experimental, as well as new media and multimedia art. Particularly, I have been exploring the use of ordinary, mundane household, industrial, and construction materials in art practice. I like to use small(er) multiples to create large scale sculpture and installations. I am actively experimenting in many areas, such as kinetic sculpture, programing and automation, 3D printing, experimental video, sound generation, and light. In my art I examine memory, mortality, heritage, tradition, and legacy, as well as other aspects of society, culture, and religion.

I am currently enrolled full time in the BFA program at FIU; applying to Masters Programs in 2018.

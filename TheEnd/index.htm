The End…, 2018

Site-specific sound and light installation
RGB and Amber LED, glass, rope, fabric, fan, speakers, sound card, media player, electrical components, pre-recorded sound material, on loop
10' x 6' x 10'
“The end...” of a period in life, a bitter sweet moment. Life goes on... The essence of the fleeting moment, a memory with multiple mixed feelings. Bitter-sweet, happy and sad, confused or nostalgic.
Two distinct installation views provide an insight on any particular memory: how we remember it vs. how it actually happened.
Subtle light and sound. Made for quiet reflection. Nostalgic moment, remembering.
At the end…, this installation is a time capsule.

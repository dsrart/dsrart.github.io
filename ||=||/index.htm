||=|| (The Norm of Existance) Series, 2025

||=|| is a series of small-scale interactive sci-art installations exploring the mysteries of reality, perception, and truth through quantum physics. By combining cutting-edge science with artistic expression, these works invite audiences to engage with the unknown, fostering curiosity and wonder. Each piece serves as both a scientific experiment and an interactive artwork, challenging viewers to reflect on the universe’s hidden forces and their relationship with them.
3 initial installations, based on the quantum theory's main concepts:
Time: “In the Land Where Time Stands Still.”
Gravity: “Ripples in the Fabric.”
Wave-Particle Duality: “Vessel of Ephemeral Truth” or “ Δ ≋ ⦙ ” or “2 < O”

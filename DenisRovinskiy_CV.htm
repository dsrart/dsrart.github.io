Summary

Emerging visual artist. Born in Soviet Union in 1980. Immigrated to the USA in 2002. Currently based in Miami.

After being primarily a photographer for a decade, last year I made an active switch to sculpture, installations, mixed media, experimental, as well as new media and multimedia art.  Particularly, I have been exploring the use of ordinary, mundane household, industrial, and construction materials in art. I like to use small(er) multiples to create large scale sculpture and installations. I am actively experimenting in many areas, such as kinetic sculpture, 3D printing, video, and sound generation, and light as part of art or as art. I am driven by memory, cultural and spiritual aspects of society, legacy, heritage, tradition, and mortality.

Enrolled full time in the BFA program at FIU and will be applying to Masters Programs in 2018.
